Service Predictor

A machine learning web application designed to identify students at academic risk and recommend the right university support services — before it's too late.

🎓
Noe Juan Yangali Ibarra
CS50x Final Project · 2026 · Harvard University Online

🎯 Project Purpose

Universities struggle to identify at-risk students before they drop out or fail. This tool uses machine learning to analyze behavioral and academic signals and flag students who need intervention — enabling advisors to act proactively rather than reactively.

🤖 The ML Model

The core is a Random Forest Classifier with 200 decision trees, trained on a synthetic dataset of 50 university students with 6 behavioral features. Cross-validated to ensure robustness.
